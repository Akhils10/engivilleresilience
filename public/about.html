About Us

About Us!

ENGIVILLE RESILIENCE: established in 2012 with the objective of bridging the gap in safety and security and ensuring a safe living society. We are leaders in safety, security, emergency and disaster management with a team of seasoned professionals with training, knowledge and experience both in Nigeria and overseas, bring a combined wealth of over 20 years of experience put together to bridge a recognized gap in the Nigerian safety, security, emergency and disaster sector.

We source a high range of products across the low and high spectrum for the satisfaction of a wide array of customers. One thing always on our mind is ‘’delivering quality products and services with precision, to ensure client satisfaction’’. As such, occupants of a building whether residential or commercial can be relaxed and confident that in the event of an emergency, whether it is fire, burglary or exposure to toxic substances (gas or liquid), there is no cause for alarm as there are provisions and solutions to alert and neutralize the threat.

In Engiville Resilience, we focus mainly on providing property safety and protection with an addition of other professional training services and solutions, intended to foster loss and harm prevention, thereby ensuring a safer, more secure and resilient society. We provide a top of the range incident and disaster prevention systems (product sales, trainings and after sales services and maintenance).

In a fast changing world of today, safety and security are an integral concern and must be taken seriously. Remember, ‘’ an ounce of prevention is worth more than a pound of cure” In the course of providing our services, we aim to instill in individuals the importance of “safety first” and the benefits of ensuring that negative events are prevented or at worse, their impact mitigated by adopting proactive measures in anticipation of any possible or foreseeable threat, hence ensuring business continuity, stability and security.

Our Mission

To instill the culture of safety and security as a fundamental part of every individual and society through trainings, awareness, and equip with relevant capacity to build preparedness for swift response to any hazardous or emergency situation through fortification of buildings.

Our Vision

To be a household and dependable brand in Nigeria and contribute to making our society safe, secure and resilient through the provision of avant-garde detection and response products and services.

Our Core Values

  1. Professionalism: timeliness, reliability
  2. Dependability: prompt response, after sales service, commitment and dedication to the needs of every client/customer
  3. Distinction and assurance: product quality and cutting-edge services.

Our Core Areas

  2. Security- cctv, access control, buglar alarm, hazard and risk assessment, business continuity management, evacuation drill,
  3. Trainings
  4. Surveillance
  5. Maintenance and services

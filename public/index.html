MISSION

To make safety and security a fundamental part of every individual and society by instilling the culture of safety and security through trainings, awareness and product installation and service provision, to build preparedness for swift response to any hazardous or emergency situation through fortification of buildings with necessary detection and response systems.

VISION

To make ENGIVILLE RESILIENCE (ER) a household and dependable brand in Nigeria and contribute to making our society safe, secure and resilient through the provision of avante garde products and services.

CORE VALUES

A Culture of honesty, sincerity and integrity, strong belief in Proffessionalism: timeliness, reliability, Dependability: Prompt response, Commitment and dedication: to the needs of every customer/client, Distinction and Assurance: quality of products and services

Welcome to Engiville Resillience

We are leaders in safety, emergency and disaster management. Engiville Resilience (ER) is a relatively new company incorporated in Nigeria in 2012 with a team of seasoned professionals with training, knowledge and experience both in Nigeria and Overseas bringing a combined wealth of over 20 years of experience put together to plug a recognized gap in the Nigerian safety, security, emergency and disaster management sector by creating a one stop shop for harnessing both international and local knowledge on safety, security, emergency and disaster management.

Nest Smoke Detector

Nest Smoke And Carbon Monoxide Detector

Nest Smoke and Carbon Monoxide Detector is a superb, smart and very intelligent safety device. It is a smoke, fire and carbon monoxide detector with many features that have never before been available.

It is time to expect more from your smoke alarm. Nest Protect has a split spectrum sensor (so detects both slow smothering fires and fast burning ones), tests itself automatically and lasts up to a decade. It also boast of being the first home alarm you can hush from your phone without any extra hardware required, and tells you what exactly is wrong(is it smoke or CO) and even alerts your phone If there’s smoke in the kitchen, it will go by saying “Heads up, there is smoke in the kitchen” thinks before it speaks: With a friendly human voice, it gives you an early warning to allow you handle a burning toast before it becomes a burning toaster. With Bluetooth and wifi, it tells you where the danger is. When one speaks, they all speak. So the alarm in your bedroom can tell you there’s smoke downstairs.

Its capability and all you need to know:

  1. 10 year battery life
  2. Identifies smoke, fire and carbon monoxide gas (CO)-a very poisonous/killer gas
  3. Voice enabled and speaks or communicates with you using real voice to tell of what the danger is or the hazard as well as its location.
  4. With the wireless and Bluetooth capability, all detectors speak and warn of the danger all together once the detector which detected the hazard sends the information to the other detectors
  5. WIFI and Bluetooth enabled
  6. Sends notification and alert to your mobile phone or communication device regardless of your location in the world
  7. Battery operated but wirelessly links all detectors together as if they are wired or looped together
  8. Addresses the situation by specifically identifying the location of the danger and communicating with the rest of the detectors.
  9. Possess motion sensor and so lights up your walk way in the absence of light
  10. Possess 5 different lighting display for different circumstances
  11. Can be mounted on the ceiling or the wall
  12. Has very good aesthetics
Meet NEST PROTECT, The smoke alarm other alarms look up to
